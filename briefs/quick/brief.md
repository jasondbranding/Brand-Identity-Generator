# Quick Brief — Canopy

## Brand Name
Canopy

## Product
A mental wellness app for remote teams. Employees get 5-minute daily check-ins, managers see anonymized team mood trends, and HR gets early burnout signals before they escalate. No therapy, no fluff — just signal.

## Target Audience
HR leaders and People Ops at remote-first tech companies. They care about retention, they're overwhelmed by reactive interventions, and they've been burned by "wellness theater" tools that employees ignore.

## Keywords
- Gentle vigilance
- Warm but data-driven
- Safe harbor
- Human signal
- Quietly powerful
